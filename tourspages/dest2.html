Kali Gandaki Rafting

 Price: $420
Duration: 2 days

The Kali Gandaki river derives its name from the goddess Kali, who is considered the Divine Mother in Hindu mythology. The river's rapids provide an adrenaline-pumping experience, offering thrills and excitement to all participants.

Originating from the Dhaulagiri and Annapurna ranges, the Kali Gandaki carves through one of the deepest canyons in the world. Along its course, you will find cascading warm-water waterfalls perfect for a refreshing break.

Why Choose Kali Gandaki Rafting?

Rafting down the Kali Gandaki is an unforgettable adventure that combines the thrill of navigating wild rapids with breathtaking scenery. Whether you are a novice or an experienced rafter, this trip is a "must-do" in Nepal. Evenings are spent around a campfire on pristine sandy beaches, allowing you to relax and bond with your rafting team.

Rafting and Kayaking

Rafting: Kali Gandaki rafting is suitable for everyone, with no prior experience needed. It is one of the most enjoyable medium-length rafting trips available in Nepal.

Kayaking: The river is perfect for intermediate kayakers and provides a fun and challenging environment for experienced kayakers. The river features multiple surf waves and holes for play boating, with one or two Class IV rapids that can be portaged if necessary.
